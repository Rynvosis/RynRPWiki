# History

The Skaagir were purpose-built as soldiers. The species that built them is not discussed in any accessible Skaagiran record — the Imperium uses the term *the Provenance* to describe this period, which manages to sound respectful of origins while containing no actual information about them. What can be inferred from the biological record is that the creators departed or perished, leaving the Skaagir with a functioning military hierarchy, operational clone foundries, and the open question of what to do with them.

The commander-caste filled the vacuum. Geneseed holders consolidated power first — controlling the means of clone production meant controlling the future population. Within a few generations, the Patrician house structure was in place. The Imperium calls this period *the Interregnum* and frames it as organic self-organisation. The more parsimonious reading is that the clones who controlled reproductive infrastructure used it to establish heritable aristocratic power and then wrote the history. Both readings are probably correct.
