# Skaagiran Imperium

*External Diplomatic Assessment — For Multiplayer Circulation*

The Skaagiran Imperium is a feudal-capitalist bio-aristocracy of small avian clones who were built as soldiers, outlived their creators, and decided the most logical response was to run the galaxy. They expand through economic integration rather than military conquest — making neighbours prosperous, making themselves indispensable, and then explaining, after the infrastructure is in place, what the arrangement actually implies. By the time anyone thinks to object, the trade lanes are already routed through Skaagiran logistics.

They are three feet tall, cold, polite, and entirely sincere about their civilisational superiority. This is perhaps the most dangerous thing about them.
