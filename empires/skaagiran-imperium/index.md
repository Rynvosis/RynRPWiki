# Skaagiran Imperium

*External Diplomatic Assessment — For Multiplayer Circulation*

The Skaagiran Imperium is a feudal-capitalist bio-aristocracy of small avian clones who were built as soldiers and outlived their creators.

They are three feet tall, cold, polite, and entirely sincere about their civilisational superiority. This is perhaps the most dangerous thing about them.
