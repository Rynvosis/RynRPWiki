# Government

## The Prime Continuant

The Prime Continuant is decanted from the Prime Strain — the original commander template, the Imperium's most protected asset. The office is framed as eternal: not the individual, but the continuity of correct administration. The Prime Strain is a military grade template. The Skaagiran head of state has always been, at the biological level, a commander. The Throne does not grieve when a Continuant dies. It decants the next one and continues.

## The Patrician Conclave

Real power operates through the Patrician Conclave — gene-aristocratic houses whose seats are proportional to their licensed clone-strain volume. These are not civilian aristocrats who maintain armies. They are commander-caste clones who also happen to control trade networks. The military and commercial functions of the Patrician class are not separate portfolios; they are expressions of the same engineered capability. Disagreements between houses are conducted through procedural manoeuvre and trade contract withdrawal rather than open conflict — not because the Patricians are averse to force, but because deploying it domestically is considered poor resource allocation.

## The Centralized State — The Seat

The Skaagiran Imperium does not distribute power evenly across its territory. It concentrates it deliberately. The capital complex — known formally as *the Seat* and informally not referred to at all, because everyone knows what you mean — is the single point through which all meaningful Skaagiran administration routes. The geneseed archive. The Patrician Conclave chambers. The Prime Continuant's residence. The oldest and most prestigious spa towers. The central registry for every clone strain license on the planet. All of it, in one place, by design.

The Seat is not a city that grew into an administrative centre. It was built as one — white stone towers housing the Conclave above, foundry infrastructure venting heat from the levels below, the archive vaults deepest of all. Regional governors administer their territories. The military enforces the Imperium's reach across the continent. But decisions of consequence — treaties, strain license disputes, succession of the Continuant — are made in the Seat, ratified in the Seat, and recorded in the Seat. The Imperium is a planetary empire. Its capital governs it like a brain governs a body: nothing significant happens without passing through it.

## The Clone Caste System

Your caste is determined by your template. Your template is determined before you are decanted. There is no appeal, because the Imperium does not consider this a decision that requires one.

### Tiers I–III: The Ruling Apparatus

**I. Prime Continuant — II. Patrician Houses — III. High Administrators**

The geneseed holders and their administrative apparatus. The Patricians own the strain licenses, run the Conclave, operate the mutagenic spa towers, and control the compound supply chains. The Administrators are the invisible machinery that turns patrician decisions into actual outcomes. They are never invited to Conclave sessions. They are always in the building.

### Tiers IV–V: The Operational Castes

**IV. Certified Elite Strains — V. Standardised Service Strains**

Elite Strains are specialist clones — officers, engineers, diplomats — small-batch, longer-lived, with names and service records. Service Strains are the labour and military backbone: high-volume, shorter operational lifespans by design. Their losses are logged as economic depreciation. Whether the Service Strains are fully aware of their projected lifespan varies by individual. Whether the Imperium considers this relevant does not.

### Tiers VI–VII: The Margins

**VI. Degraded Utility Strains — VII. Null-Strain / Untempered**

Degraded Strains are cost-optimised or aging-template production runs: shorter lifespans, reduced baseline, deployed to the most attritive environments. The Untempered are vat errors — template failures that fall outside every administrative category the Imperium uses to manage everything else. They are not citizens. They are not slaves. They exist in the margins of Skaagiran cities doing work no licensed strain has been assigned. Their numbers grow proportionally with production scale. The Imperium notes that this remains within acceptable parameters.

## The Mutagenic Spas

Officially: performance optimisation facilities. The Imperium frames them as engineering discipline applied to the body — maintenance and calibration of a system. This framing is consistent, coherent, and leaves out most of what is actually happening.

The Spas are chemically volatile enhancement chambers where Patrician-caste Skaagir receive compound cocktails that push cognitive performance and social acuity beyond their already-elevated template baseline. The aristocracy runs permanently overclocked. The effects are real: a recently-treated Patrician has a processing speed that is perceptible in conversation as something between impressive and uncomfortable. They also smell faintly antiseptic and metallic — an immediate status marker among Skaagir that is never discussed and universally understood.

The compounds metabolise. The edge is sustained, not inherent, which means ongoing access to supply chains controlled at the raw material level by the Progenitor houses — the most powerful families in the Conclave. The geneseed controls your birth. The spa compounds control your continued relevance. The Progenitors control both. Service and Line Strain Skaagir have their own compound culture — cruder, harsher, pre-combat stimulant doses. The chemistry is recognisably related. The quality differential is enormous.
