# Biology

The Skaagir are compact alpine avians — dense-plumed for cold nights, hollow-boned, roughly three feet in height. Their creators added a secondary pair of arms below the primary set: smaller, more dexterous, built for precision instrument work and close-quarter combat in the kind of confined corridors you find aboard ships. Their spatial cognition is exceptional. Their physical durability is not, which the Imperium has always compensated for through engineering rather than endurance.

Commander-caste Skaagir are measurably larger and cognitively faster than the lower tiers. Whether this is intrinsic to the species or a product of the genetic engineering applied to upper-grade templates is a question the Imperium finds unremarkable. The answer, in either case, would be the same: they were made to lead. They lead.
